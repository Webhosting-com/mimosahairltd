Our story

About Mimosa Hair

A salon built on passion, purpose, and an unwavering commitment to your hair health.

Est. 2025

Where expertise meets care

Mimosa Hair was born from a vision to create a salon experience unlike any other — one where the health of your hair is treated with the same importance as its beauty. Founded by Victoria Kate, a senior hair stylist with years of experience, every aspect of Mimosa Hair reflects a genuine passion for the craft.

We believe that great hair starts with great care. That's why we exclusively use vegan, non-toxic, and eco-friendly products — because we care about what goes on your hair as much as we care about the finished result.

From creative cuts and expert colour to transformative extensions and compassionate hair loss support, our team of skilled specialists are here to create your dream hair, your way.
